About Blackwellen

A studio for enduring professional brands

Blackwellen was founded to answer one question: how do you launch a portfolio of portals that stays relevant long after the first release? We combine research, product strategy, and brand architecture to give communities a connected experience from day one.

Every engagement pairs creative direction with operational blueprints—covering governance, monetisation, and service delivery so teams can scale with confidence.

Enterprise portals

A constellation of brands shaping the professional economy

Edulure, Gigvora, and Fixnado share a modular technology core, a unified design language, and orchestrated data layers—enabling communities to learn, collaborate, and transact without friction.

Visit Fixnado Visit Edulure Visit Gigvora

Edulure is an online learning community and course marketplace—we call it a learn space. It brings together communities, live classrooms, tutors, e-books, courses, video courses, drip content, assignments, course progression tracking, and monetization for instructors through sales, community subscriptions, and hourly tutoring.

Explore Edulure

Academic orchestration

Adaptive learning, live classrooms, and accreditation workflows.

Community intelligence

Engagement analytics and mentorship programs guided by data.

Gigvora is a professional community for freelancers, companies, agencies, headhunters, job seekers, clients, mentors, and volunteers. The platform includes a live feed community, creation studio, high-level dashboards, projects, gigs, and an experience launchpad for career changers, plus auto-matching for freelance projects, a jobs board with interviews, a kanban organization system, calendars for bookings, and speed networking for meaningful connections.
