Privacy Policy

Data protection at Blackwellen

Blackwellen protects information with the diligence expected of enterprise partners. This policy outlines how we handle data when you browse our website or communicate with our team.

1. Controller details

Blackwellen operates from the United Kingdom and is responsible for any personal data processed in connection with this website. Contact us at contact@blackwellen.com for privacy-related enquiries.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact data provided when you email our team.
  • Usage data from analytics tools that help us improve site performance.
  • Communication records maintained to deliver accurate follow-up and governance.

3. How we use data

We use data to respond to enquiries, maintain security, and optimise the digital experience. Processing is based on legitimate interests or consent when required.

4. International transfers & retention

Data may be stored with trusted providers offering safeguards aligned with UK GDPR. We retain information only as long as necessary for the stated purposes.

5. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data. You also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies to understand performance trends. You can adjust preferences through your browser settings.

7. Updates

We review this policy to maintain compliance with evolving regulations. Material updates will be published on this page with a revision date.
